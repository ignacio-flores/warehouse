Minimalist Examples: Table-First Layout

These are three stripped-down versions of the table-first approach. All keep the tray pinned open on wide screens and show a draggable divider. Focus on density, clarity, and whether the top bar feels appropriately lean.

1

Balanced top bar

Counts and scan state stay visible. Tray holds filters, benchmark, actions, and help.

2

Very lean top bar

Top bar shows only title, counts, and apply. Scan and tools move fully into the tray.

3

Sectioned tray

Same as option 1, but the tray is broken into compact collapsible blocks for more order.
